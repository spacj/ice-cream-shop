# Pistacchio Utrecht - Premium Gelato Shop

## Brand Positioning
**Artisanal Premium Gelato Boutique in Utrecht**
- Hand-crafted daily using only the finest ingredients
- Sicilian pistachio as signature ingredient
- Modern Dutch aesthetic meets Italian craftsmanship
- Destination experience, not just a shop

## Design Strategy

### Visual-First Approach
- **Hero Section**: Large, immersive product photography/video
- **Product Gallery**: Showcase gelato in beautiful settings
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Show craftsmanship and quality
- **Customer Experience**: Atmosphere and ambiance
- **Location Pride**: Utrecht's design culture reflected

### Color Psychology for Premium Positioning
- **Primary**: Creamy white/ivory (clean, premium, minimalist)
- **Accent 1**: Deep pistachio green (#4A7C59) - sophisticated, natural
- **Accent 2**: Warm gold/caramel (#D4A574) - luxury, warmth
- **Secondary**: Soft charcoal (#3D3D3D) - modern, refined
- **Supporting**: Cream, beige, soft grays - elegant neutrals

### Typography Strategy
- **Display**: Modern serif (Playfair/Bodoni Moda) - luxury, premium
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Poppins/Inter) - modern, Dutch minimalism

### Layout Strategy
- **Generous whitespace** - breathing room, premium feel
- **Large product images** - 60-70% of hero, above fold
- **Minimal text** - let visuals speak
- **Content sections**: 
  1. Product Hero (video/carousel)
  2. Featured Flavors (3 columns, large images)
  3. Craftsmanship Story (image + short text)
  4. Process/Quality (visual journey)
  5. Shop Location/Experience
  6. Customer Reviews
  7. Contact with location map

### Key Changes from Previous Design
- Remove colorful animations - too playful
- Increase whitespace and minimalism - premium
- Emphasize product photography - 60% of page is visuals
- Remove excessive gradients - use solid colors with purpose
- Modern Dutch design: clean, functional, beautiful
- Focus on craft story - why Pistacchio matters
- Utrecht identity: modern, design-forward, quality-conscious
